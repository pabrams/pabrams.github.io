At the Department of Citizenship and Immigration, transitioned the department from manual, UI-based release procedures to fully automated Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment workflows, significantly reducing deployment times and human error.

- Decommissioned over-engineered legacy C# desktop applications for release management, replacing them with lightweight PowerShell scripts using the TFS Client APIs
- Guided teams in migrating from legacy XAML builds to modern, scriptable build definitions
- Improved build reliability and maintainability across the organization
